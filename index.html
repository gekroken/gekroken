Methodology

In this research we compared types of brand engagement of Dubai and Paris in Instagram from customer and brand side. For both sides, with typologies deduced from academic references, each Instagram engagement was analyzed and categorized. Customer side: By using the application program interface (API) data of both cities, we measured the number of interactions (likes and comments) and typology covering 100 most liked posts for each city brand. Brand side: The official Instagram groups (IG) of the cities are also explored.

We have calculated the engagement rate per post in each IG by formula: Engagement rate = (likes + comments) / followers / number of posts x100% And applied typology to every post in the groups. IG accounts covered in total 200 post, consequently 200 posts were extracted from the API data. Next we evaluated the quality of the users engagement. We took 1 post of each account in order to analyze the way consumers engage with different types of brands behavior. We took as a standard of "quality comment" (QC) a comment with more than 1 word not including tags and emo.
